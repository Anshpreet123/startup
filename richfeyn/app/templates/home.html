FAQs


How does it save money ?

There are many ways in which you can save money

  • App alerts you when you are food items are about to lose its freshness. This helps you consumes food at its best reducing health care costs due to consumption of less fresh food and waste 0 food and money spent on it due to expiry .
  • App ensures you reach your nutrition targets and stay healthy while your nutritionist / fitness trainer/ doctor can access your food consumption and contact if there are any changes required or make changes themselves (with your permission). This helps health care providers do more in less time and give quality feedback as they have historical data and save health care costs incurred due to improper nutrition .
  • App helps you purchase just the right quantity of food items so you don't end up overstocking which saves you money and avoids wastage due to expiry of unused items .
  • App helps you purchase just the right quantity of food items so you don't end up under-stocking and you run out of food items and since you need it urgently you might end up buying from wrong brand/company/s .

    • How does it help me if I prefer shopping offline ?

  • Grocery list will be prepared based on your available kitchen inventory without any errors. It gives you the freedom to go shopping anywhere anytime without having to plan in advance for grocery shopping.
  • It will remind you of existing food items to use when they are about lose its freshness (expire or come close to use by date) .
  • It will help share your consumption details (with nutrition tracker feature) to your personal fitness trainer/doctor/nutritionist .

    • Why do I need this as I am using transparent containers where I can see when the food is running out?

    Our containers are also translucent where it can seen if the food is running out but there are close to 100 ingredients in the kitchen are in different quantities and they are consumed at different rates so you have to place order often. For a regular grocery shopping, you have to make a list of all the ingredients of all the recipes you want to eat. We all do this rough calculation manually. It is prone to errors. With RichFeyn solution, all this is automated meticulously precisely while also reducing the number of shopping trips or orders

    What happens when I travel out of town?

    When you go out of town, the kitchen items about to expire can be generated on the app for disposal or donation and when you are back, grocery lists will be prepared for you to order such that everything will be restocked at the right time in the right quantity as if you never left town

    How are the devices powered?

    Each container is powered by 2 AAA batteries and can run for 12 months without replacement. We will send you new batteries for replacement when you are about to run out.

    How does meal planner work?

    App you plan meal for a date or week by choosing recipes and respective ingredients get added to the cart. On confirmation, they arrive just in time to ensure they are as fresh as possible when you are about to cook. Recipes can be customised by user to enable the right ingredients are added to the cart when these recipes are chosen to add to cart. You can share your meal plans with your fitness trainer/nutritionist/family doctors

    Can I order different food items from different sellers ?

    Yes, you can. You can also set your preferences for different sellers, brands and delivery time slots

    How does it help me shop for parties/festivals ?

    App lets us choose number of people and recipes for the parties/festivals. It checks for the quantity of the required ingredients in your kitchen and prepared the grocery shopping list in the cart page.
